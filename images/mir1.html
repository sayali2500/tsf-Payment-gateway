Miracle Foundation India is non-profit organization supporting orphaned and vulnerable children.

We know the very best thing for a child is to be part of a loving family—it’s where they thrive. That’s why everything we do as a children’s organisation is designed to accomplish just that.

Our goal is a loving family for every child and the end of the need for Child Care Institutions (CCIs). We believe that, together, we can change the way we care for children and help to get them into stable, loving, nurturing environments.

Thanks to you, we are currently seeing our methodology used in over 300 CCIs in India and are helping over 15,000 orphaned and vulnerable children in need to thrive.

These children deserve the very best. A way out of their past of separation and a way forward into their bright future. We believe in partnerships, and with generous gifts from people like you, we can make their dream of living with a family a reality.
